MERIDIANPRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED · 19 JUL 2026 · APPLIES TO drewosi.github.io/lucid-engine

The short version: Meridian runs entirely in your browser. We operate no servers and therefore cannot see your files, your questions, your AI conversations, or your API key. The only personal data that can ever reach us is an email address you choose to submit to the waitlist — and only once that form is switched on. Everything else stays on your machine or travels directly between your browser and Anthropic under your own account.

§01Who we are

This site and the Meridian workbench are operated by [ENTITY] ("we", "us"). Contact for anything in this policy, including privacy requests: drewosipenko@gmail.com.

§02What the architecture makes impossible

Meridian is a static site with no backend. There is no database, no account system, no server-side logging of your activity by us. Concretely, we never receive:

  • the project files you load into the workbench — they are read into your browser tab's memory only;
  • your questions and the AI's answers;
  • your Anthropic API key.

This is a property of the architecture, not just a promise: you can verify it in the public source code or in your browser's network inspector.

§03Data stored in your browser (localStorage)

Meridian stores a small amount of data in your own browser's localStorage so the pages can function. This data never leaves your device unless noted below, and you can delete all of it at any time.

The workbench settings panel includes a single button that removes every Meridian key from your browser. Loaded project files and conversation history are held in memory only and are gone when the tab closes.

§04Data sent to Anthropic (your AI provider)

When you use the workbench, your browser sends your question, the project files you selected, and your API key directly to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com). That processing happens under your own Anthropic account and Anthropic's own terms — we are not a party to it, do not sit in the middle of it, and cannot access it.

Review how Anthropic handles API data in the Anthropic Privacy Policy and their Commercial Terms. You choose what to send: only the files you check in the context panel are transmitted.

§05The waitlist (the only data we can receive)

The landing page has an optional waitlist form (email address plus an optional tier interest).

  • Current state: until the form's transmission backend is configured, entries are stored in your browser's localStorage only and are never transmitted anywhere. The form says so beneath the field.
  • When live: entries are sent to Formspree, a form-processing service acting as our processor, and arrive in our inbox. The form's caption changes automatically to disclose this.
  • Purpose and retention: we use your address for exactly one thing — contacting you about Meridian's beta. No newsletters you didn't ask for, no resale, no ad pixels. We keep it until the beta communication purpose is over or you ask off the list, whichever comes first.
  • Opt out: reply to any message we send, or email us, and the address is deleted.

§06Cookies and analytics

We set no cookies. There are no advertising trackers, no social pixels, no fingerprinting.

If we enable analytics, it will be GoatCounter, a privacy-respecting, cookieless counter that stores no personal data and requires no consent banner. Analytics are currently disabled, and will never run on the workbench page. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages; GitHub may log standard web-server data (such as IP addresses) to operate the service — see the GitHub Privacy Statement. The workbench page additionally loads one styling asset (Tailwind CSS) from Tailwind’s CDN (cdn.tailwindcss.com); like any static-asset host, that CDN may log standard request data such as IP addresses when the page loads. It receives none of your files, questions, or keys.

§09Children

Meridian is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has submitted an email address, contact us and we will delete it.
