ENGINE

The combination of hospitable clarity, visible engineering, and cinematic depth.

A bright, calm, immediately legible surface built on machinery so precise and so alive that discovering it produces awe. The surface welcomes everyone. The depth rewards obsession.

Nothing ships that a first-time user can't navigate. Mystery lives in the experience, never in the architecture.

LAW 02

LAW 03

Depth is a reward, not a requirement

The mode rule

Purpose decides the door. Products, tools, dashboards open light. World-sites, launches, cinematic marketing open dark. Both modes always built, always switchable. Darkness is a purpose, never the lazy default.

Contrast

AA minimum, AAA body text. Atmosphere comes from light and depth, never lowered text contrast â€” gray-on-gray sophistication is banned.

Spacing & grid

4px base unit, 12-column civic grid, full-bleed ceremony breaks left deliberately and briefly. Density is a feature: dense and beautifully organized beats sparse and empty. Optical alignment beats mathematical.

Shape & borders

Radii 2â€“6px on controls; sharp structural corners welcome. Hairline 1px borders mark real boundaries. Borders + light over drop shadows. No pill cards.

Light & shadow

Shadows are physics â€” shallow, tight, only where elevation is real. Golden-hour warmth for human moments, overcast calm at rest, neon only after dark and only when earned.

Texture & materials

Subtle film grain keeps engineered surfaces from sterility. Materials read real: glass refracts, metal reflects, particles obey â€” or deliberately defy â€” physics. No glassmorphism wallpaper.

Imagery

Authored cinematic photography or the system's own procedural output. No stock, no corporate lifestyle, no AI-generated filler â€” nothing a viewer could clock as generated, not authored.

3D & generative

The crown jewel, spent like one: procedural, physical, or interactive â€” a real system on display with its engineering discoverable. In products, ceremony lives in the seams: loading, empty states, onboarding, transitions. Working screens stay instrument-calm.

Icons & gradients

Icons are instrument markings: geometric, hairline-consistent, on-grid. Illustration only as engineering drawings â€” diagrams, cutaways, exploded views. Gradients only as light falloff, never decoration.

Interaction Language

Register

Supernatural â€” particles, dissolves, materialization, morphs, trails â€” but always triggered by and proportional to what the user did. Motion never plays at the user.

Scroll

Native physics never hijacked. Scroll may drive reveals, particle assembly, camera moves â€” position and speed always belong to the user.

Cursor & sound

Design Tensions

// not compromises â€” the identity itself

Warmth + Virtuosity

The trade nobody makes. Rule: welcome first, awe seconds later. Never intimidate at the door.

Density + Calm

Cyberpunk's information, Overwatch's readability. Rule: any screen may hold a lot, but must answer where-am-I / what-can-I-do / where-next in under 3 seconds.

Supernatural motion + Physical response

Rule: impossible behavior, believable causality.

Light world + Dark ceremony

Rule: purpose decides the door â€” tools open light, spectacles open dark, both always exist.

Beyond-human system + Human empathy

Rule: the system may feel more advanced than a human could build; the experience must feel like a human anticipated you.

Mystery + Clarity

Rule: users may wonder how it works; they must never wonder how to use it.
