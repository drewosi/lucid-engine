MERIDIAN // BEFORE YOU BEGIN

A real instrument, in free beta.

  • It runs entirely in your browser. We operate no servers. Your files, questions, and key are never sent to us.
  • Bring your own API key. Anthropic by default; OpenAI or a custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint in settings. Requests go directly from this browser to the provider under your account — usage is billed by the provider to you, at their rates.
  • Your key stays local. It is stored in this browser's localStorage and sent only to the selected provider's API endpoint. Use a spend-limited key.
  • AI output can be wrong. Answers and traces are machine-generated and may be inaccurate or incomplete. They are not professional advice. Verify before you rely on them.
